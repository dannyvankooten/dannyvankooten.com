Hi. I'm Danny, a nerd in his late twenties.

More products followed while I worked from warmer parts of the world for a few years. These days, I am usually working from home in The Netherlands while trying to be a father for my 2 daughters.

I am currently working on Koko Analytics: a privacy-friendly analytics plugin for WordPress.
